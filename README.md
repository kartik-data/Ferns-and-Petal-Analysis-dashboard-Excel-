<h1 align="center">Ferns & Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard</h1>

<p align="center">
A complete sales analytics project built using Microsoft Excel to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📖 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project focuses on analyzing sales data from <b>Ferns & Petals (FNP)</b>, a gifting and e-commerce company that sells products for various occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries, Diwali, Holi, Valentine's Day, and Raksha Bandhan.
</p>

<p>
The objective was to clean the raw dataset, perform exploratory data analysis, calculate business KPIs, and build an interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders understand revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and delivery efficiency.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Problem Statement</h2>

<p>
Business managers often have large amounts of sales data but struggle to extract meaningful insights from it.
This project aims to answer critical business questions such as:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Which months generate the highest revenue?</li>
<li>Which product categories contribute most to sales?</li>
<li>Which occasions drive customer purchases?</li>
<li>Which cities place the highest number of orders?</li>
<li>Which products perform best in terms of revenue?</li>
<li>How efficient is the delivery process?</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation</h2>

<p>
Before analysis, the dataset was thoroughly cleaned to ensure data quality and accuracy.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Removed duplicate and inconsistent records.</li>
<li>Verified revenue and order-related fields.</li>
<li>Converted date columns into proper date format.</li>
<li>Created Month and Year fields for trend analysis.</li>
<li>Calculated delivery duration using Order Date and Delivery Date.</li>
<li>Standardized category and occasion names.</li>
<li>Prepared Pivot Tables for dashboard reporting.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</h2>

<p>
Several analyses were performed to understand business performance from different perspectives:
</p>

<h3>Revenue Analysis</h3>

<ul>
<li>Analyzed monthly revenue trends.</li>
<li>Identified peak and low-performing months.</li>
<li>Calculated total business revenue.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Category Analysis</h3>

<ul>
<li>Compared revenue across product categories.</li>
<li>Identified the highest revenue-generating category.</li>
<li>Measured category-wise order distribution.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Occasion Analysis</h3>

<ul>
<li>Examined customer purchasing behavior during special occasions.</li>
<li>Compared revenue generated during festivals and celebrations.</li>
<li>Determined the most profitable occasion.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Product Analysis</h3>

<ul>
<li>Ranked products based on generated revenue.</li>
<li>Identified top-performing gift products.</li>
<li>Compared product contribution to overall sales.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Geographical Analysis</h3>

<ul>
<li>Analyzed order distribution across cities.</li>
<li>Identified cities with the highest demand.</li>
<li>Compared regional purchasing patterns.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Dashboard Features</h2>

<ul>
<li>Interactive KPI Cards</li>
<li>Revenue by Category Analysis</li>
<li>Monthly Revenue Trend Visualization</li>
<li>Orders by Category Distribution</li>
<li>Revenue by Occasion Analysis</li>
<li>Revenue by Product Analysis</li>
<li>City-wise Order Distribution</li>
<li>Date and Occasion Filters (Slicers)</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🔑 Key Business Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Total Revenue generated exceeded ₹35 Lakhs.</li>
<li>Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets emerged as major revenue contributors.</li>
<li>Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest occasion-based revenue.</li>
<li>August recorded the highest monthly revenue.</li>
<li>Magnam Set was the highest revenue-generating product.</li>
<li>Average delivery time remained around 5.5 days, indicating efficient order fulfillment.</li>
<li>Imphal, Kavali, and Dhanbad showed strong customer demand.</li>
</ul>
